WONDER LA

WONDERLA is the largest AMUSEMENT PARK near the city and is one of the best places to visit in Banglore. The people of Banglore love driving to wonderla with their family and friends.This place offers me a gala time to its visitors with more than 50 dry and wet rides.Also a variety of water rides are available. Best time to visit wonderla is in Summer. Rain Dance, WavePool, Mixer, Twister etc., are the some of the must see rides.
